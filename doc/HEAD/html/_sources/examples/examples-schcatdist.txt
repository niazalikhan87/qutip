Wigner and Q-function distributions for a Schrodinger-Cat state
===============================================================
  
   
Calculates the Wigner for a Schrodinger Cat-state composed of two coherent states :math:`\alpha_1=-2-2j`, and :math:`\alpha_2=2+2j`.
