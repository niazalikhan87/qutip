Vacuum Rabi oscillations in the Jaynes-Cummings model
=====================================================

Illustrates the vacuum Rabi oscillations in the Jaynes-Cummings model with
dissipation.

This examples introduces the use of bosonic operators (:func:`destroy`, for the 
annihilation operator) and how to setup the Jaynes-Cummings model.
