Von-Neumann entropy of a binary mixture of |up> and |down> states
==================================================================
     
Calculates the entropy of a mixture of :math:`a\left|up\right>\left<up\right| + (1-a)\left|down\right>\left<down\right|` states as the mixing parameter :math:`a` is varied from 0 to 1.
